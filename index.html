Helping You Love Dental Care through Patient-Focused Dentistry

In addition to possessing the skill, technologies, and treatments to serve your smile effectively, Dr. Gerosa believes that exceptional dentistry includes a foundation of trust through kindness during a detailed consultation. When visiting us for care, our dentist takes the time to get to know you and your smile personally, rather than diving right into treatment.

Once our Westchester and Culver City, CA, area dentist has answered your questions, addressed your concerns, and helped ease your fears, he will work closely with you to craft a treatment plan that is all your own. Treatment recommendations are always based on your current oral health, and designed to protect and maintain your healthy teeth and gums. We do not over-diagnose or push unnecessary procedures.

Some of our available services include:

  • Dental Cleanings & Examinations
  • Dental Implants & Restorations
  • Crowns, Bridges & Dentures
  • Dental Emergencies
  • Sedation Dentistry
  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Invisalign Clear Aligners
  • Root Canal Therapy
  • Wisdom Teeth Extractions
  • Gum Disease Treatment

Our general dentistry offerings include digital x-rays and thorough yet gentle examinations, educating patients about our findings along the way. We also offer tooth-colored composite fillings for a more uniform smile, and beautiful porcelain materials for our crown, bridge, and denture restorations. For particularly anxious patients, we provide nitrous and oral conscious sedation options to help further maximize your comfort.
