Meet Dr. Warren Gerosa, DDS

A gentle, experienced dentist serving Los Angeles for over 30 years.

Dr. Warren Gerosa, DDS

USC School of Dentistry Graduate • 30+ Years of Experience • General Dentist

Dr. Warren Gerosa earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Southern California School of Dentistry, one of the nation's top dental programs. With more than 30 years of experience serving the Los Angeles community, Dr. Gerosa has built a reputation for gentle, compassionate, and high-quality dental care.

Dr. Gerosa is, above all else, a nice guy. While he is sure to perfect your smile and leave you feeling more than satisfied with your dental care, his kind, gentle demeanor are what patients speak to most often. Maybe that's why some of Dr. Gerosa's patients remain his closest friends.

Dr. Gerosa stays current with the latest dental techniques and technologies through continuing education, ensuring his patients receive the most advanced care available. He is an active member of the American Dental Association, California Dental Association, and the Los Angeles Dental Society.

From routine cleanings to complex restorative procedures, Dr. Gerosa's gentle approach and attention to detail ensure the best possible experience and results for every patient who walks through the doors of Dove Dental Spa.
