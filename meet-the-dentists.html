Dr. Warren Gerosa, DDS

USC Dental Graduate | 30+ Years of Experience

Dr. Gerosa is a USC dental graduate with more than 30 years of experience in the LA dental world. Dr. Warren Gerosa is, above all else, a nice guy. While he is sure to perfect your smile and leave you feeling more than satisfied with your dental care, Dr. Gerosa's kind, gentle demeanor are what patients speak to most often.

Maybe that's why some of Dr. Gerosa's patients remain his closest friends. To see for yourself what a great dentist and person Dr. Gerosa is, make an appointment at Dove Dental Spa today.

Dr. Gerosa and his team are dedicated to providing personalized, compassionate care to every patient who walks through their doors. From routine cleanings to complex restorative procedures, his gentle approach and attention to detail ensure the best possible experience and results.
