What Is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry focuses on improving the appearance of your teeth, gums, and smile. Unlike general dentistry which prioritizes oral health, cosmetic treatments are designed to enhance aesthetics while also improving function and confidence.

At Dove Dental Spa, we combine artistry with advanced dental technology to create natural-looking results that complement your unique facial features.

Who is it for? Anyone who feels self-conscious about their smile. Whether your teeth are discolored, chipped, crooked, gapped, or unevenly shaped, there is a cosmetic solution that can help.

Conditions Cosmetic Dentistry Can Solve

  • Discolored teeth that do not respond to over-the-counter whitening
  • Chipped or cracked teeth from accidents or wear
  • Crooked or misaligned teeth that affect your smile's symmetry
  • Gaps between teeth that you want to close
  • Uneven or worn-down teeth from grinding or aging
  • Old or mismatched fillings that need replacement

Our Cosmetic Dentistry Treatments

Porcelain Veneers

Veneers are ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of your teeth to correct color, shape, size, and alignment. They look and feel like natural tooth enamel and can transform your smile in as few as two visits.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Our professional-strength whitening treatments are safe, effective, and deliver visible results in a single appointment. Unlike drugstore products, our formulas protect enamel and minimize sensitivity.

Dental Bonding

A tooth-colored resin is applied and sculpted to repair chips, cracks, gaps, and discoloration. Bonding is typically completed in one visit and offers beautiful results at an affordable price point.

Smile Makeovers

A complete smile transformation combining multiple treatments tailored to your goals. During your consultation, we design a personalized plan that may include veneers, whitening, bonding, and more to create your ideal smile.
