What Is General Dentistry?

General dentistry focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of common oral health conditions. Regular dental visits are essential for maintaining healthy teeth and gums and catching potential issues early before they become serious problems.

At Dove Dental Spa, our general dentistry services are delivered in a relaxing, spa-like environment. We believe preventive care should be comfortable and even enjoyable.

Why regular visits matter: The American Dental Association recommends checkups every six months. Regular visits can detect cavities, gum disease, oral cancer, and other issues at their earliest, most treatable stages.

Our General Dentistry Services

Comprehensive Dental Exams

Our thorough examinations include visual inspection, digital X-rays, oral cancer screening, gum disease evaluation, and bite analysis. We assess every aspect of your oral health to create a personalized care plan.

Professional Teeth Cleanings

Professional cleanings remove plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing cannot reach. Our gentle hygienists use advanced instruments to clean and polish your teeth while ensuring your comfort throughout.

Fillings

We use tooth-colored composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth. Unlike traditional metal fillings, our composite materials bond directly to the tooth and require less removal of healthy structure.

Preventive Care

Fluoride treatments, dental sealants, and customized home care instructions help protect your teeth between visits. We also provide night guards for patients who grind their teeth.
