About Dove Dental Spa

Where peaceful dentistry meets spa comfort in Westchester, Los Angeles.

At Dove Dental Spa, we understand that there are probably lots of things you would rather do than go to the dentist. That's why we do everything in our power to make the whole process as easy and comfortable for you as possible.

Beginning with our secure appointment scheduling, we take the hassle out of having to remember to call. Once you arrive, we greet you with cold cucumber water, soothing waterfalls and a relaxing massage chair — making you remember why the word "spa" is in our name.

But perhaps what distinguishes us the most from other dental practices is our warm staff. As one Yelper recently wrote, "One of the hygienists even massaged my head to help with the nervousness!" And that is Dove Dental Spa in a nutshell.

"One of the hygienists even massaged my head to help with the nervousness!"

We invite you to experience the Dove Dental Spa difference. Whether you need a routine cleaning, cosmetic enhancement, or restorative treatment, our team is here to make your visit as pleasant and comfortable as possible.
