Insurance & Financing

Quality dental care should be accessible. We work with you to make treatment affordable.

Insurance

We accept most major dental insurance plans and will verify your benefits before your visit so there are no surprises. Our team handles the paperwork so you can focus on your smile.

Accepted plans include: Aetna, Cigna, Delta Dental, MetLife, Guardian, Humana, Blue Cross Blue Shield, UnitedHealthcare, and many more.

Not sure if we accept your plan? Contact us and we will check for you.

Financing Options

CareCredit

CareCredit is a healthcare credit card designed specifically for health and wellness expenses. It offers flexible payment plans including interest-free financing for qualified applicants. You can use CareCredit for treatments not fully covered by insurance, including cosmetic procedures.

In-House Membership Plan

No insurance? No problem. Our annual membership plan covers preventive care at a predictable monthly rate and includes discounts on additional treatments. Ask us about our membership options.

Flexible Payment Plans

We offer customized payment plans for major treatments. We believe cost should never be a barrier to the dental care you need and deserve.

Verify Your Benefits

Not sure about your coverage? We will verify your benefits for free.
